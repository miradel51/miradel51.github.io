🧑‍🎓 Biography
---------

Dr. Mieradilijiang Maimaiti is currently an Associate Professor at the [School of Computer Science and Technology](http://it.xju.edu.cn/), [Xinjiang University (XJU)](https://english.xju.edu.cn/). Before joining XJU, he served as a Senior Algorithm Engineer in Natural Language Processing (NLP) at the [Language Technology Lab](https://damo.alibaba.com/labs/language-technology), [Alibaba DAMO Academy](https://damo.alibaba.com/).

He received his Ph.D. from the [Department of Computer Science](https://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/csen/) at [Tsinghua University (THU)](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), where he was advised by [Prof. Maosong Sun](https://nlp.csai.tsinghua.edu.cn/staff/sms/) and co-advised by [Prof. Yang Liu](https://nlp.csai.tsinghua.edu.cn/~ly/) in the [THUNLP Lab](https://nlp.csai.tsinghua.edu.cn/). His research interests include natural language processing, machine translation, semantic representation learning, and large language models.

Since 2016, he has been conducting research on machine translation, with a particular focus on neural machine translation (NMT) for low-resource and morphologically rich languages, including minority languages. His work has also explored domain adaptation, transfer learning, and data augmentation for low-resource machine translation, as well as morphological analysis for agglutinative and inflectional languages.

Before joining THUNLP, he obtained both his bachelor's and master's degrees from the [School of Information Science and Engineering](http://it.xju.edu.cn/) at [Xinjiang University (XJU)](https://english.xju.edu.cn/), majoring in Computer Science and Software Engineering, respectively. During his master's studies, he conducted research on computational morphology, speech recognition, speech synthesis, and morphological analysis under the supervision of academician [Prof. Wushouer Silamu](https://ysg.ckcest.cn/html/details/3943/index.html). Before pursuing his Ph.D., he also gained extensive experience in software development for desktop, web, and mobile platforms.

Currently, his research focuses on multilingual and multimodal large language models for low-resource languages, with particular interests in text generation, reasoning, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), tool learning, semantic retrieval, and multimodal learning.

🔎 Research Interests
------------------
Mieradilijiang has a broad research interest in language technology:
* __Natural Language Processing__
  * __Machine Translation__
    * __Low-Resource Languages Scenario__
    * __Data Augmentation__
    * __Transfer Learning__
    * Domain Adaptation
    * Prior-Knowledge
  * Morphological Analysis & __Word Segmentation__
  * __Semantic Representation__: Sentence Retrieval / Knowledge Retrieval
  * Sentiment Classification: Few-Shot Learning & Zero-Shot Learning
  * Multilingual Processing

* __Large Language Models (LLMs)__
  * Pre-training for Low-Resource Languages
  * Instruction Tuning / Supervised Fine-tuning (SFT)
  * Post-training and Alignment
  * Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

* __Multimodal and Low-Resource NLP__
  * Multimodal Fusion
  * Multimodal Data Construction

* __Evaluation__
  * Benchmarking for Low-Resource Language Models

* Information Retrieval
  * Cross-Language Information Retrieval
* Machine Learning for NLP
* NLP Applications
